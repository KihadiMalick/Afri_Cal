#### 4.3.5 Exceptional architecture of the Silhouette feature

The **Silhouette** sub-feature of Secret Pocket benefits from a **protection architecture among the strictest in the health applications industry**, combining exclusive local storage, hardware encryption and biometric authentication.

**Exclusive local storage of photos**

The photos you take to track your silhouette evolution are:

- **Stored exclusively on your device's memory**, in the LIXUM application's private folder (system isolation zone managed by iOS/Android)
- **Never transmitted to LIXUM, Supabase, Anthropic servers** or any other subprocessor
- **Encrypted locally with AES-256-GCM** (Advanced Encryption Standard 256 bits, Galois/Counter Mode), with an encryption key derived from your biometric authentication
- This encryption key is **stored in the Secure Enclave** (iOS) or hardware **Android Keystore** (TEE — Trusted Execution Environment), inaccessible even to LIXUM

This architecture ensures that even in the event of a theoretical security incident on LIXUM servers, **no silhouette photo could be compromised** — these photos simply do not exist on our servers. And even if someone accessed the LIXUM application's private folder on your phone, they would only find unreadable encrypted files without your biometrics.

**Practical consequences of this choice**

- If you lose your device, uninstall LIXUM, or change phones without transferring your data, **your silhouette photos are definitively lost**
- LIXUM cannot restore these photos in case of loss (LIXUM has never had knowledge of them)
- You are solely responsible for backing up your photos via your operating system tools if you wish (it being understood that standard iCloud / Google Drive backups do not include the application's private folder)

This is the **assumed price of absolute anonymity** on this category of particularly sensitive data.

**Local extraction of measurements**

To enable you to track your physical evolution over time, LIXUM extracts **locally, directly on your device**, certain measurements from your photos:

- Contour and dimension measurements
- Anatomical ratios
- Numerical morphometric data

The extraction is performed by an algorithm embedded in the application, without transmission of photos to an external server. **Only this numerical data** (numbers) is then transmitted and stored on LIXUM servers, never the source images. This data allows you to visualise your evolution in the form of graphs and statistics.

**One-time consultation by ALIXEN ("Get ALIXEN's opinion")**

If you wish to benefit from a more in-depth analysis by ALIXEN of your physical evolution, you can at any time click the **"Get ALIXEN's opinion"** button from your Silhouette page in Secret Pocket.

This action constitutes a **one-time and explicit consent** authorising you to temporarily transmit your photos to ALIXEN for analysis:

- The transmission is framed by the subprocessor Anthropic (see Privacy Policy section 7.2)
- The photos are **not retained after analysis** by Anthropic
- **No copy is stored** on LIXUM or Anthropic servers
- The consent is valid for this analysis only; you must give it again for each new request
- You can revoke this consent at any time by no longer using the feature

You retain **total control**: without explicit action on your part, the photos remain exclusively on your device.

#### 4.3.6 Photo guide and complementary protections (Silhouette)

To further reinforce the protection of your morphological data, LIXUM provides a **photo guide** each time you add a Silhouette photo. This guide notably recommends:
